Qr-X A Fortress For Digital Assets

Qr-X is a comprehensive data security platform that prioritizes security and efficiency. Providing a robust solution for safeguarding sensitive information across various platforms, from small businesses to enterprise and Industrial Internet of Things (IIoT) environments.

Solution Summary

In an era where data breaches and cyber threats are increasingly sophisticated, Qr-X technology offers a fortress for digital assets. Imagine a world where digital files are protected and defended by cutting-edge technology. Technology that uses intelligence-based shields combined with unique proprietary encryption that can defeat conventional and sophisticated threats, including quantum computing and Artificial Intelligence.

  • A robust solution for safeguarding sensitive information
  • Seamlessly operates across desktop, server, and mobile platforms, ensuring the data is protected wherever it resides
  • A low-impact, easily configured, secure experience utilizing pre-built management applications
  • Designed to work in concert with current processes and workflows, not disrupt them
  • Built for performance
  • Protect the data without sacrificing speed or efficiency
  • Unique Qr-X Keys and Qr-X Vault specifications offer unparalleled portability, supporting everything from individual files to entire folders, all without compromising security

Features

  • Strong Encryption – Qr-X utilizes industry-standard AES encryption with proprietary algorithms, designed to be hack-resistant, quantum, and AI-resistant
  • Secure Data Access and Sharing – Unique Qr-X keys are used for secure data access and sharing, with support for multiple data elements within a single protected Qr-X Vault
  • High Performance – Qr-X is designed to protect data without sacrificing speed or efficiency
  • Cross-Platform Versatility – Qr-X operates seamlessly across desktop, server, and mobile platforms
  • Additional Security Features
    • In-memory data protection
    • Hack mitigation
    • Tamper-proofing of code and data
    • Basic governance: expiration and read-only access
